An integrated system of 3 modules

Setting the new frontier for 21st century home kitchens

Storage module

A special made refridgerator that works seemlessly with the rest of the machine. Here, users would load their ingredients into the right storage slots as prompted by the instructions on their mobile device or our tablet.

Prepping module

Upon user's commands from the app, the ingredients get to be transported from storage module to prepping module. Here, all the ingredients will get processed. The prepping module is able to perform your average home made food prepping autonmatically, such as peeling, cutting, seeds removal, washing, egg cracking, seasoning,etc. At the end of each prepping cycle, it will clean itself.

Cooking module

Prepped ingredients arrive here and get cooked. This module will be able to perform all the cooking functionalities, ranging from sauting, stir firing, roasting, frying, steaming, baking, boiling, broiling to grilling.
